✦ FAQ

Got Questions?

How far in advance should I book?+
We recommend booking 2–3 days in advance for standard setups. Same-day bookings are possible for simpler packages — just WhatsApp us!
Do you set up at any location in Pune?+
Yes! We set up at homes, apartments, hotel rooms, terraces, restaurants, farmhouses and outdoor venues across Pune. If you can give us access, we can make it magical.
How do I keep it a secret?+
We coordinate entirely with you. You distract the birthday person while our team sets up quietly. We'll give you a time estimate so you know exactly when to arrive.
When will I receive my event photos?+
Edited photos are delivered via a private Google Drive link within 24–48 hours of your event. For the Memories section, enter your booking ID or phone number to access them.
What are your payment terms?+
A 50% advance confirms your booking. The balance is paid on the event day after setup. We accept UPI (scan our QR code), bank transfer, and cash. Advance is non-refundable within 48h of the event.
Can I have a custom theme?+
Absolutely! Share your Pinterest board, colour palette, or any reference via WhatsApp and our team will customise every element to match. No theme is too specific for us.
